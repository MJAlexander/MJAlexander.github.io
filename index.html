Monica Alexander

About Me

I am a second-year PhD student in the Demography Department at the University of California, Berkeley. This academic year I am taking courses in the Statistics Department. I am interested in applications of statistics to social issues such as maternity, migration and economic outcomes.

I work as a Graduate Student Researcher at the Center on the Economics and Demography of Aging, and as a Consultant to the UNICEF Technical Advisory Group. In the past I worked at the at the ANU's Centre for Aboriginal Economic Policy Research and at the Reserve Bank of Australia.

Outside of work and study I am interested in photography and music.
