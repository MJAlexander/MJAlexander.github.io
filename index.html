Monica Alexander

About Me

I am a PhD student in the Demography Department at the University of California, Berkeley. My research interests include statistical demography; mortality modeling; small-area population issues; and the demography of Indigenous populations.

I work as a consultant to the World Health Organization. In the past I have worked at the Center on the Economics and Demography of Aging, as a Consultant to the UNICEF Technical Advisory Group, at the ANU's Centre for Aboriginal Economic Policy Research and at the Reserve Bank of Australia.

Outside of work and study I am interested in photography, cycling, and music.
